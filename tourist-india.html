Taj Mahal — Agra

One of the world’s most famous monuments, built as a white marble mausoleum by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan. Best visited at sunrise or sunset.

Amber Fort & Jaipur — Rajasthan

Jaipur, the Pink City, offers palaces, forts, and vibrant markets. Amber Fort is a must-see hilltop fort with royal architecture.

More

Kerala Backwaters — Alleppey

Peaceful canals, lagoons, and houseboat stays. Ideal for relaxed travel, scenic views, and experiencing local life.

More

Goa Beaches

Famous for golden beaches, seafood, and a relaxed coastal vibe. Offers both lively party scenes and quiet stretches.

More

Varanasi — Ghats on the Ganges

One of the oldest living cities, known for its spiritual ghats, rituals, and rich cultural history. Best for cultural and spiritual travelers.

More

Ladakh — Mountains & Pangong Lake

High-altitude desert landscapes, dramatic mountains, and clear blue lakes. Great for trekking, road trips, and photography.

More

Hampi — Karnataka

Ancient temples and boulder-strewn landscapes from the Vijayanagara Empire. A UNESCO World Heritage site popular with history lovers.

More

Khajuraho — Madhya Pradesh

Known for intricately carved temples dating to medieval times. Famous for stunning stone sculptures and architectural detail.

More

Andaman Islands

Tropical islands with white-sand beaches and clear water. Great for snorkeling, diving, and unwinding by the sea.

Ranthambore — Tiger Reserve

One of India’s top national parks for wildlife safaris, known for its tigers and diverse wildlife. Book safaris in advance.
